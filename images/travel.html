Cusco, Peru

While visiting Cusco, you can revel in the city's famous Inca engineering, stonework, picturesque narrow streets, ornate cathedrals and colorful, lively and lavish artwork. Do not miss out on a stroll through the main Plaza de Armas to see the Cusco cathedral and churches as well as the amazing architecture.
